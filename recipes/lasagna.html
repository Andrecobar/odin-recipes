meat lasagna

Lasagna is undoubtedly a favorite dish for the whole family, it is perfect for when you are cooking for a lot of people.

ingredients

  • 3/4 lb. extra-lean ground beef
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1-1/2 dried oregano leaves
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 container (16 oz.) BREAKSTONE'S 2% Milkfat Lowfat Cottage Cheese or KNUDSEN'S
  • 1/4 cup grated KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 (7 oz.) KRAFT 2% Milk Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, divided

steps to follow

  1. Heat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Brown the meat with the garlic and oregano in a large saucepan. Stir in spaghetti sauce; simmer 5 min., stirring occasionally. Remove from heat; add the tomatoes. Mix the egg, cottage cheese and Parmesan cheese well.
  3. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce over bottom of 13x9-inch pan. sprayed with cooking spray. Top with layers of 3 pasta sheets, half of the cottage cheese mixture, 1/2 cup of the mozzarella and 1 cup of the sauce. Repeat the layers. Top with remaining pasta and sauce; cover it up
  4. Bake the lasagna for 30 min. or until hot. Top with the remaining mozzarella. Bake uncovered 5 min. or until cheese melts. Let it rest 5 min. before serving.
